How to buy BAYC?

  1. Copy contract address, this can be found on our Bscscan page or right here: 0xc4f5424ef52499fa496a07f3fe9daab88553d4c3
  2. Connect your wallet : Connect the wallet of your choice to Pancakeswap. Then, paste the contract address in the select a currency menu (click "Import" then on BAYC )
  3. Set Slippage and Confirm Click the sliders right under the exchange on Pancakeswap and set your slippage tolerance to 30% (this is necessary due to BADAPES being in its early stages). Next, input however much of any token you wish to trade and proceed with the transaction. BADAPES ($BAYC) can be traded to and from universally!
