BadApes Tokenomics

Passive Income System
2% of every transaction with $BADAPES is deducted and redistributed to holders. It means that just by holding it you will see the number of tokens increasing automatically everyday as the community transacts.
Deflationary Currency
An additional 1% of the amount of all transactions is taken out of circulation, meaning that our token supply is constantly shrinking and making your $BADAPES increase in value with time. We have already started with 39% of the supply burned.
Anti-Whale-Bot Trap
Everyone is expected to make profit but we want profit to be for everyone. $BADAPES is coded in a way that prevents big wallet holders (whales) from trying to manipulate the price in their favour or from dumping the token by selling out.

Bad Apes Commitment

Building a community-owned platform for Dapps with breakthrough technologies and new economic models presents an opportunity to change the crypto industry forever. It also presents a set of complex challenges that have never been solved before.
To help guide us as we map out this new frontier of decentralized apps are 5 key commitments that are reflected in our work, and in the community itself.
  1. Community owned
    Today’s apps are built on economic models that are unsustainable in the future for many. We’re dedicated to rewriting the rules and building an entirely new type of economic platform that sets community ownership, participation, and growth as its starting point—and its end. By rewarding developers, community members, and publishers who contribute to the network’s health, a new era of community-focused app economies can emerge.
  2. Open & Interoperable
    We believe Bad Apes community-owned platform must be inclusive, independent, and interoperable. That’s why we’re weaving cross-chain and cross-platform functionality into the fabric of our platform, erasing any barriers to entry for community members, and ensuring that our platform will continuously improve and serve the community as new protocols, tools, and standards arise.
  3. Decentralized
    Centralized platforms inevitably create a virtual ceiling and often end up taking opportunities from, and limiting growth, for the rest of the community through monopolistic behavior. As our technology matures and adoption increases, we commit to transition to a fully decentralized platform that’s owned, controlled, and nurtured by the community at large. In doing so, developers, players, and publishers of all sizes will be able to reap the benefits of community economics without having to trust a centrally managed platform.
  4. Governance
    Our aim is to create a transparent, intuitive, and sensible governance framework that ensures no one person or group can control the Bad Apes platform or network, and that actions which are in the best interests of the larger community are rewarded. We’re working tirelessly to find the right rules and mechanisms necessary to create such an unprecedented but vital governance system.
  5. Accountability
    We care deeply about people. That’s why we’re building a platform that we believe will forever change the crypto industry in positive ways—better business models for developers, new types of apps for fans, and community economics that benefit everyone. As such, we’re committed to long-term investments in building the proper technology and infrastructure necessary for the platform and community rather than seeking any form of short-term financial gain.
